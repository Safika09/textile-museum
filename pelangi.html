Pelangi Gallery

The rainbow is an arc of light with bands of various colors appearing in the blue sky.
In traditional textiles, "kain pelangi" (rainbow cloth) is known as "kain benang raja," a fabric with a variety of colors.
The soft hues include green, pastel, yellow, and blue.
The Rainbow Gallery symbolizes the colorful bands of the rainbow,
which can be associated with the diverse ethnic groups in Malaysia, each rich in their own textile heritage.

This gallery showcases a curated collection of heritage items such as the diversity and evolution of batik,
collections from the Chinese community, Baba and Nyonya, featuring intricate silk and gold thread embroidery, beadwork,
as well as the ethnic textiles of Sarawak and Sabah, known for their refinement and intricate motifs.
