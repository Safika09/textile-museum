Diploma Show Machang ART Inspiration 2013

Tarikh: 25 April 2013 - 05 May 2013

Lokasi: Galeri Saindera


Machang Art Inspiration (MARi) is a newly rebranded exhibition showcasing the works of final-year diploma students
from the Faculty of Fine Arts and Design, involving the Department of Fine Metal Design and Textile Design.
The inaugural MARi 2013 exhibition, held at the National Textile Museum, featured 57 student artworks
complemented by pieces from several final-year lecturers.
