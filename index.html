GALLERY OF NATIONAL TEXTILE MUSEUM We welcome you to explore the great Gallery of National Textile Museum
MALAYSIA

The National Textile Museum takes you to explore the rich diversity in Malaysian society. The museum traces the trends and developments of textiles that have characterized and shaped the lifestyle of Malaysian society since prehistoric times to the present day. Visitors can visit four galleries showcasing a collection of renowned textiles, accessories and clothing. The multimedia presentation also highlighted the techniques of textile production in Malaysia.
