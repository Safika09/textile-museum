Pohon Budi Gallery

The Pohon Budi, also known as the Tree of Life or the Banyan Tree,
symbolizes the beginning, serving as the opening act on the stage of life.
Pohon Budi, a large tree offering shelter, represents history, the essence of life, and the civilization that unfolds
like a tree starting from a seed, growing, and bearing fruits in the form of shapes and colors that can be utilized.

The Pohon Budi Gallery showcases the history of textiles,
beginning from prehistoric times and tracing the development of textiles through trade routes.
Also exhibited are tools, materials, and traditional techniques of textile production,
including weaving, embroidery, batik-making, couching, crocheting, and beadwork.
