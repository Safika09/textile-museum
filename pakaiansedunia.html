Pakaian Sedunia Anak Patung

Tarikh: 09 July 2013 - 18 August 2013

Lokasi: Galeri Saindera


A collection of dolls showcasing ethnicities from various countries
such as Japan, New Zealand, and Spain is on display at the National Textile Museum in Kuala Lumpur.
This exhibition serves as evidence of the skills and traditions of diverse ethnic groups from around the world.
It also focuses on the traditional attire worn by local communities,
reflecting the textile history associated with specific cultures.
