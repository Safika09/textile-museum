Pameran Seni Alat Tenun

Tarikh: 28 Disember 2012 - 10 Mac 2013

Lokasi: Galeri Saindera


Colorful, vibrant, and beautiful textiles are still largely produced in Southeast Asia,
using only the simplest and most easily accessible tools.

The Weaving Tools Art Exhibition focuses on weaving tools and equipment found in Southeast Asia,
explaining the use of threads, dyeing and warping methods, types of weaving,
as well as weaving techniques.
