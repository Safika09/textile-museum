Objectives Of Gallery


VISION

Towards a museum that provides a unique and exhilarating experience for visitors to the museum through the excellence of collections, exhibitions and excellent programs from professional staff.


MISSION

1. Nurturing, demonstrating and interpreting a collection of traditional textiles and personal jewelry.


2. Producing and documenting textile and personal jewelry investigations.


3. Provide a variety of special exhibitions on an ongoing basis.


4. Offering a variety of educational programs that will introduce textiles and personal jewelry to visitors.


5. Becoming a top destination for tourists.


OBJECTIVES

Research, maintain, document and exhibit the country's textile heritage collections to foster awareness and understanding of values, ethics and aesthetics in the production, use, wearing, culture and development of textiles.
