Teluk Berantai Gallery

Teluk Berantai is a prominent motif in Malay songket weaving.
Originating from scattered floral patterns, this motif forms a harmonious design when connected through
weaving, resembling linked squares that join one flower to another.
Teluk Berantai symbolizes the closeness and unity of the Malay community in producing enduring artistic heritage.

The Teluk Berantai Gallery showcases the richness, intricacy, and beauty of Malay heritage collections,
including tekatan (embroidery), kelingkan (metallic thread embroidery), woven fabrics, limar cloth, songket,
limar songket tenggarung, telepuk fabric, inscribed cloth (kain berayat), and various traditional attire.
